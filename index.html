About the practice

A family-run practice with real heart

Dr Gurvinder Singh Thiara and his wife Winny took over Wollaton Road Dental Practice in 2010. His calm, caring nature has converted many nervous patients into happy regular attenders — and some of those patients have been coming since 1978.
